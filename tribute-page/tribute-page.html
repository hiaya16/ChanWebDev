Dr. José Rizal

Philippines National Hero

Timeline of Dr. Jose Rizal's life

  • 1861: Born in Calamba, Laguna
  • 1882: Traveled to Spain for higher studies
  • 1887: Published "Noli Me Tangere"
  • 1891: Published "El Filibusterismo"
  • 1892: Exiled to Dapitan, Mindanao
  • 1896: Returned to Manila and was executed
